Sunday's Sermon

Bible Reading
  1. Luke 18:1-8
Sermon Summary

  • If an unjust judge can act on a request from the persistent widow, how much more can our God avenge for His own!
  • We should pray continuously for for it is the source of our breakthrough.
  • Communion with God will open doors that nobody else can open.
  • Communion with God is the very purpose of prayer.
  • The highest call of prayer is communion with God.
  • The veil at the temple tore to symbolize that we now have direct access to Christ.
  • Some of us want blessings from God, yet we are not willing to commune with Him.

    Insight Corner

    Some things will not happen unless we pray. Why should we pray if it doesn’t make any difference? James 4:2tells us “you do not have because you do not ask.” To say it another way, if we don’t use the key the door won’t open. God can change our circumstances if we will use the key and call out to Him for help.

    What is Communion?

    Communion is God's communication to us coupled with our response to him — all in such a way that he's glorified and we're glad. Communion hopefully does happen when we do the Lord's Supper, but it's not limited to that event.
